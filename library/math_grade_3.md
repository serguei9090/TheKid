# Grade 3 Mathematics: Multiplication and Fractions
*Context: Mathematics, Grade 3, Arithmetic, Fractions, Geometry*

## 1. Multiplication and Division
- Multiplication is repeated addition (e.g., 2 x 3 is 2 + 2 + 2).
- The symbol for multiplication is 'x'.
- Division is sharing equally or repeated subtraction.
- The symbol for division is '÷'.
- Multiplication and Division are opposite actions.
- Any number multiplied by 0 is 0.
- Any number multiplied by 1 stays the same.

## 2. Understanding Fractions
- A 'Fraction' represents a part of a whole.
- The 'Numerator' (top number) tells us how many parts we have.
- The 'Denominator' (bottom number) tells us how many equal parts make one whole.
- '1/2' is one-half.
- '1/3' is one-third.
- '1/4' is one-fourth.
- Fractions can be compared and placed on a number line.

## 3. Geometry and Measurement
- 'Area' is the space inside a flat shape.
- 'Perimeter' is the distance around the outside of a shape.
- A 'Polygon' is a closed shape with straight sides.
- A 'Quadrilateral' is a polygon with four sides.
- Mass is measured in Grams or Kilograms.
- Liquid volume is measured in Liters.

## 4. Problem Solving
- Reading word problems helps us use math in real life.
- Estimating means finding a value close enough to the right answer.
- Rounding to the nearest 10 or 100 makes math easier.
