# Descriptive Logic and Opposites (Grade 1)
*Context: Logic, Adjectives, Opposites*

## 1. Comparing Sizes
- 'Big' describes something large.
- 'Small' describes something tiny.
- 'Big' is the opposite of 'Small'.
- 'Long' describes something with great length.
- 'Short' describes something with little length.
- 'Long' is the opposite of 'Short'.

## 2. Colors and Feelings
- 'Red', 'Blue', 'Green', and 'Yellow' are names of colors.
- 'Happy' is a feeling of joy.
- 'Sad' is a feeling of sorrow.
- 'Happy' is the opposite of 'Sad'.
- 'Fast' describes moving with speed.
- 'Slow' describes moving without speed.
- 'Fast' is the opposite of 'Slow'.

## 3. Position and Location
- 'Up' is towards the sky.
- 'Down' is towards the ground.
- 'Up' is the opposite of 'Down'.
- 'In' is inside a space.
- 'Out' is outside a space.
- 'In' is the opposite of 'Out'.
- 'On' means sitting on top of something.
- 'Under' means sitting below something.
- 'On' is the opposite of 'Under'.

## 4. Logical Grouping
- Apples and Bananas are types of fruit.
- Cats and Dogs are types of animals.
- Happy and Sad are types of feelings.
- Red and Blue are types of colors.
- Running and Jumping are types of actions.
