# Phonics and Letter Sounds (Grade 1)
*Context: Grammar, Phonics, Sounds*

## 1. Single Letter Sounds
- The letter 'A' makes the sound /æ/ as in 'apple'.
- The letter 'B' makes the sound /b/ as in 'bat'.
- The letter 'C' makes the sound /k/ as in 'cat'.
- The letter 'D' makes the sound /d/ as in 'dog'.
- The letter 'E' makes the sound /ɛ/ as in 'egg'.
- The letter 'F' makes the sound /f/ as in 'fish'.
- The letter 'G' makes the sound /g/ as in 'goat'.
- The letter 'H' makes the sound /h/ as in 'hat'.
- The letter 'I' makes the sound /ɪ/ as in 'igloo'.
- The letter 'J' makes the sound /dʒ/ as in 'jam'.
- The letter 'K' makes the sound /k/ as in 'kite'.
- The letter 'L' makes the sound /l/ as in 'leaf'.
- The letter 'M' makes the sound /m/ as in 'moon'.
- The letter 'N' makes the sound /n/ as in 'nest'.
- The letter 'O' makes the sound /ɒ/ as in 'octopus'.
- The letter 'P' makes the sound /p/ as in 'pig'.
- The letter 'Q' makes the sound /kw/ as in 'queen'.
- The letter 'R' makes the sound /r/ as in 'rabbit'.
- The letter 'S' makes the sound /s/ as in 'sun'.
- The letter 'T' makes the sound /t/ as in 'tent'.
- The letter 'U' makes the sound /ʌ/ as in 'umbrella'.
- The letter 'V' makes the sound /v/ as in 'van'.
- The letter 'W' makes the sound /w/ as in 'watch'.
- The letter 'X' makes the sound /ks/ as in 'box'.
- The letter 'Y' makes the sound /j/ as in 'yo-yo'.
- The letter 'Z' makes the sound /z/ as in 'zebra'.

## 2. Consonant Digraphs
- The combination 'sh' makes the sound /ʃ/ as in 'ship'.
- The combination 'ch' makes the sound /tʃ/ as in 'chin'.
- The combination 'th' makes the sound /θ/ as in 'thin' or /ð/ as in 'this'.
- The combination 'wh' makes the sound /w/ as in 'whale'.
- The combination 'ph' makes the sound /f/ as in 'phone'.

## 3. Vowels
- A, E, I, O, U are called vowels.
- Short vowels are loud and quick.
- Long vowels usually sound like the letter's name.
