# Sight Words and Basic Vocabulary (Grade 1)
*Context: Grammar, Vocabulary, Sight Words*

## 1. High-Frequency Sight Words
- 'The' is a common sight word used to point to a specific noun.
- 'Of' is a common sight word used to show belonging or connection.
- 'And' is a common sight word used to join words or sentences.
- 'A' is a common sight word used before a singular noun.
- 'To' is a common sight word used to show direction or action.
- 'In' is a common sight word used to show location inside something.
- 'Is' is a common sight word used to describe a state of being.
- 'You' is a common sight word used to address the listener.
- 'That' is a common sight word used to point to something specific.
- 'It' is a common sight word used to replace a thing or animal.

## 2. Common Nouns (People, Places, Things)
- A 'Boy' is a male child.
- A 'Girl' is a female child.
- A 'House' is a place where people live.
- A 'School' is a place where people learn.
- An 'Apple' is a fruit that is often red or green.
- A 'Ball' is a round object used for playing games.
- A 'Cat' is a small furry animal that meows.
- A 'Dog' is a furry animal that barks and is a pet.
- A 'Tree' is a tall plant with leaves and branches.
- A 'Book' is something you read to learn or enjoy stories.

## 3. Action Verbs
- To 'Run' is to move quickly on feet.
- To 'Jump' is to push off the ground into the air.
- To 'Eat' is to take in food.
- To 'Sleep' is to rest with eyes closed.
- To 'Read' is to look at words and understand them.
- To 'Write' is to use a pen or pencil to make words.
- To 'Play' is to do something for fun.
- To 'Talk' is to use words to speak.
- To 'Listen' is to use ears to hear.
- To 'Look' is to use eyes to see.
